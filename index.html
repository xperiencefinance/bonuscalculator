Submission Format — Best Practices
📅
Submit Early in the Month
Policy must be delivered AND placed in force to earn commission. Submit at month start to ensure it settles within that billing cycle — delays push FYC into the next month.
📋
Complete Replacement Forms on the Spot
For any replacement, have the form signed by both parties at point of sale. Missing forms = delayed or forfeited commissions.
🎯
Track Lives Separately from Premium Volume
Lives Bonus is per policy count, not premium size. A $200/month and a $2,000/month policy both count as 1 life. Never undercount small policies.
📊
Review Trailing 12-Month Total Monthly
Volume Bonus is computed on trailing 12 months. Know your number going into each month so you can push extra cases when near a threshold.
🔄
Batch Cashbuilder Additions with Annual Reviews
When doing annual policy reviews, bring a Cashbuilder application to every meeting. This turns a retention call into a new FYC opportunity.
Replacement Commission Scale

When replacing an existing policy, your FYC is restricted based on the age of the policy being replaced.

0 – 3 Years Old
0% AVOID
Zero commission on the replacement. Don't do it unless there's a strong client benefit — and even then, you earn nothing. Compliance will also flag these.
3 – 8 Years Old
40% CAUTION
You earn 40% of normal FYC. Only worth it if the client clearly benefits, or if you engineer a significant premium increase on the new policy.
Over 8 Years Old
80% VIABLE
You earn 80% of FYC. Pair with a $25+/month premium increase to earn 100% FYC on the increased portion — significantly lifting your total earnings.
The Premium Increase Play

Regardless of policy age, if the new policy premium exceeds the old one by at least $25/month, you earn FULL First Year Commission on the increase portion.

Always aim to increase the premium by at least $25/month when replacing any policy — it unlocks full commissions on the increase and significantly lifts total earnings.

Replacement Checklist
✅ Confirm age of existing policy before submitting
✅ Calculate whether a $25+ premium increase is feasible
✅ Complete and sign Replacement Form with client at point of sale
✅ Attach replacement form to application — missing forms delay commissions
✅ Document client benefit clearly (Compliance Unit reviews all replacements)
✅ Never replace a policy under 3 years old — zero earnings and risk compliance flag
